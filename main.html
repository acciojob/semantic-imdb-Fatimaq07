Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a coming-of-age Bollywood film that follows the journey of Kabir (Bunny), a free-spirited traveler who dreams of exploring the world, and Naina, a shy and studious girl who discovers herself during a trekking trip. The story explores themes of friendship, love, dreams, and choices. As Bunny chases his ambitions, he realizes the value of relationships and emotional connections. The film beautifully portrays how people grow, change, and find balance between passion and companionship over time.
